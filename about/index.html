About Savingator

Fast, free, and privacy-focused savings and deposit planning tool.

Our Mission

Savingator’s mission is to help users plan and manage their savings efficiently. Our tool calculates the exact amount you need to save for your financial goals while taking interest and tax into account — all on your device, without sharing your data online.

How It Works

All calculations happen directly in your browser. Your data never leaves your device. You can plan your savings for different timeframes and deposit frequencies safely and securely.

Why Choose Savingator

  • Privacy-first — your financial information stays on your device
  • Fast client-side processing
  • Accurate calculation including interest and taxes
  • Supports multiple savings frequencies and timeframes

Disclaimer

Savingator provides estimated calculations for personal financial planning purposes only. Interest rates and taxes are approximate and may vary by country, bank, or individual circumstances. Always verify with official financial institutions.
